History of Chulls

THE UNDOCUMENTED DAYS

The first days of server included the Original Seven: NuggetBro, WamBamSam, UberAndre, LemonDude123, YaBoyPayden, BestBeanBoy, and SassyLemon0127. Spawn included a central array of bases and an ice highway terminal. This would soon determine the main structure of city planning you see on Chulls. The first great bases were constructed in this era, with the Temple and Treehouse being the most notable. The sugarcane farm along the ice highway was one of the largest farms at the time. YaBoyPayden was notorious for his wifi troubles. Later on in these days, WamBamSam, NuggetBro, SassyLemon0127, and LemonDude123 made the First Arch.

CLANS

This era of Chulls history followed directly after the First World. This was the precursor to the Golden Age and many important events occured that still are known to this day. In these days, new players joined in the form of Prii5m and Scott's Legion. Scott, the tyrant of Chulls, was most successful in this era and recieved his first ban. YaBoyPayden is responsible for Scott's tyranny, inviting him to the server in the first place. However, Prii5m and NuggetBro, menaces of their own degree, eventually overpowered Scott and his devious ways. This era was known for the lack of builds and decentralization, as well as the Netherrack Wastes outside of spawn. This world was also the main time SaucySacrifice and Zorg were active.

THE GOLDEN AGE

The Golden Age was easily the most documented and most popular era in Chullian history, containing deep lore and well-preserved accounts of many events. For this era, two worlds will be covered by a player-to-player basis, recapping the major events. Two worlds will be referred in this chapter: Skinny Penis Town world (ab. SPT) and Mt. Chulls World (ab. Mt.C), named after the most famed bases respectively. Other bases include The Greater Mt. Chulls Area, Chico's Dong, The Cottage, and the Windmill.

NUGGETBRO: THE MENACE

NuggetBro set out to be the most powerful person on the server at all times, often bringing along Prii5m when active. On the SPT world, he would often harass the inhabitants of Skinny Penis Town with his riches and power. He then proceded to drive the remnants of Scott far away before his final, permenant ban. In the Mt.C world, he waged conflicts with Chico and Prii5m against Chullia, a coalition government between SassyLemon0127, powerfulwhale, LemonDude123, and YaBoyPayden. However, with overwhelming resource superiority, NuggetBro won every conflict he entered. The following is an exerpt from the Battle of Mt. Chulls:

YABOYPAYDEN: THE CULTIVATOR

Ultimately a force for good after severing ties with Scott, YaBoyPayden would prove to be a mighty builder and creator. His ability to band together groups for a build project was a large reason the original method of city planning remained on Chulls. His works in Skinny Penis Town and the Greater Mt. Chulls area are certianly his greatest yet, as his chapter is far from over.

People of Skinny Penis Town, clockwise: Mr__shAle (top), BestBeanBoy (right), WamBamSam, YaBoyPayden, NuggetBro, and LemonDude123 (left).

ITZ.WI11: THE MANIPULATOR

After cultivating the NW Outpost, itz.wi11 proved himself a worthy builder and capable player. However, his exploits were exposed after he took advantage of a dupe. While the decision was made to keep him on the server, significant faith was lost in the admins. This event ultimately led to the reset of the Mt.C world, marking an end to the Golden Age.

CHICO_GUAPO: THE MEMER

Undoubtly the most highly memed (Mr. Shale a close second) player, Chico is known for his outrageous build and bipolar attitude. He would do things on a whim, making him the most entertaining to follow in Chulls. He has built countless phallic structures and memes, but his skill as a builder is proved nonetheless.

WAMBAMSAM: THE INDIFFERENT

WamBamSam is widely known for his builds and long time loyalty to Chulls. His great builds came after the Golden Age era, but he started off with the Cottage on SPT. He is a feirce competitor and protector of his lands, but he is known for his neutrality in conflicts.

THE FIRST DARK AGE

The first dark age followed the Golden Age. This was a period were the server was mostly dead, mostly taken up by speedrunning. There was a quick world that include SassyLemon0127, LemonDude123, NuggetBro, and Prii5m. This, however, was short lived.

THE PORT CITY

This era came directly after the Dark Ages. The resurgence of a consistent player base including WamBamSam, LemonDude123, SassyLemon0127, BestBeanBoy, and UberAndre led to the creation of a central city highly influenced by Skinny Penis Town. WamBamSam created a gorgeous lighthouse overlooking a wide network of docks (Dirty Docks), connecting to a Port City (upgraded village) and Snow Village across the ocean. This time was tranquil and peaceful, and some of the most enjoyable time that was had on the server with such a small playerbase.

THE 1.18 WORLD

This was the largest resurgence in players since the Golden Age. The amount of players exceded the Golden Age. This era was shortlived however, as rife competition at spawn led to new players not being able to get a foothold. However, this world marked a return to the famed ice highways and megabases Chulls is known for. One such megabase, LemGetLol, featured the famed Chico_Guapo, NuggetBro, an inactive player Veggieelol, and LemonDude123. This was home to the Second Arch, mountain bridges, the MTV Logo, the second Dirty Dock, and mountain top cottages. WamBamSam also created a replica of the famed Treehouse, paying homage to his first ever build on the server. YaBoyPayden's friend JakeYouOut, who we haven't covered, also returned.

THE SECOND DARK AGE

This period was the longest and darkest time in Chullian history. The server, at most times, was not even operational and as such the playerbase died. This would last until LemonDude123's last-ditch effort to save Chulls with the New Era

THE NEW ERA

This is the most recent and ongoing era in Chulls. There is already a small build and some people have gotten on, including SpeedyB2k17, NuggetBro, WamBamSam, LemonDude123, and BestBeanBoy. This is Chullia's final push against the death of server.
